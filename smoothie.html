Smoothie

An easy and delicious drink that will keep you energized throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 cup unsweetened milk of any kind
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • Honey, to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend together all the ingredients.
  2. Pour it into a glass and enjoy!
