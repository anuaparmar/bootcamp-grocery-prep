Oatmeal

A quick and filling meal that can be made a number of ways.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup water or milk
  • Toppings of your choice (i.e. fresh fruit, nuts, honey, peanut butter, chocolate chips)

Instructions

  1. Combine oats and water or milk in a pot over medium/high heat.
  2. Stir until the mixture begins to boil, then lower the heat and continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes while stirring occasionally.
  3. The oatmeal is ready when it is creamy and the oats have absorbed most of the liquid. Place the oatmeal in a bowl, add the toppings of your choice, and enjoy!
