Avocado Toast

An easy-to-make snack that will always be tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (i.e. whole wheat, white)
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tbsp of pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the two slices of bread.
  2. Mash and spread the avocado on the toast.
  3. Add pepper, if desired, and enjoy!
