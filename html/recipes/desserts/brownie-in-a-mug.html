Brownie in a Mug

A gooey, chocolatey dessert.

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp oil (i.e. coconut oil, canola oil, sunflower oil)
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp chocolate chips
  • 1 pinch(es) of salt

Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients in a microwaveable mug and mix it together.
  2. Microwave on high for 1 1/2 minute and enjoy!
