S'mores

A warm and gooey treat.

Servings

3

Ingredients

  • 6 graham crackers, broken in half
  • 1 chocolate bar, divided into fourths
  • 3 marshmallows

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Add half the graham crackers to a parchment paper-lined baking sheet pan.
  3. Place one piece of chocolate and one marshmallow on top of each graham cracker.
  4. Bake for 3-5 minutes or until the marshmallows puff up and turn golden brown.
  5. Place the remaining graham cracker on top and enjoy!
