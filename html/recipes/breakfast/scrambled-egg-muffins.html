Scrambled Egg Muffins

An on-the-go breakfast guaranteed to kick-start your day.

Servings

6

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked chopped spinach
  • 1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup diced tomatoes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Place paper liners in 6 cups of a muffin tin.
  2. In a large bowl, crack the eggs and whisk until smooth.
  3. Add the spinach, cheese, tomatoes, salt, and pepper and stir.
  4. Divide the egg mixture evenly into the muffin cups and bake for about 15 minutes or until the eggs are set.
  5. Let it cool down and then enjoy!
