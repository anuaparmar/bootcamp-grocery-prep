Guacamole

A quick and easy snack that is easily portable.

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • 1/2 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lime
  • 1 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Combine the mashed avocado, chopped tomato, onion, and minced garlic in a large bowl.
  2. Squeeze and add the lime to the bowl.
  3. Add salt, place in the fridge for half an hour, and enjoy with chips!
