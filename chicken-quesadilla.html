Chicken Quesadilla

A cheesy, delicious hot pocket of joy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas (i.e. flour, whole wheat)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 oz diced, cooked chicken

Instructions

  1. Place the one tortilla on a large pan and top with cheese and chicken.
  2. Place the other tortilla on top and cook on medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side or until lightly brown.
  3. Let the quesadilla cool down, cut, and enjoy!
