Rice Bowl

A quick and easy meal that can be easily customized for all.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb boneless chicken
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 shredded carrot
  • 2 cups shredded purple cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 thinly sliced avocado
  • 1 cup raw edamame
  • 1/3 cup of teriyaki sauce
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken on a foil covered sheet pan and brush with teriyaki sauce.
  2. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350 degrees or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  3. Let the chicken cool down and then cut into slices.
  4. In a bowl, place 1 cup of brown rice, top with chicken, carrot, cabbage, avocado, edamame, and green onion.
  5. Top with sesame seeds, if desired, and enjoy!
