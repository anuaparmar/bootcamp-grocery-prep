Salad

A light and healthy meal that is easily customizable.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of leafy greens (i.e., Romaine lettuce, Iceberg lettuce)
  • 1 handful sliced red onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan shavings
  • Croutons (optional)
  • Italian dressing (optional)

Instructions

  1. Chop the leafy greens, red onion, and slice the cherry tomatoes.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add croutons and Italian dressing, if desired. Enjoy!
