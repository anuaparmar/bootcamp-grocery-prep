Rice Krispies

A nostaligic dessert from everyone's childhood.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
  • 1 package marshmallows
  • 3 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large pan.
  2. Add marshmallows to the pan and stir until melted.
  3. Combine Rice Krispies cereal with the marshmallow-butter mixture.
  4. Place on a large sheet pan and let it cool down.
  5. Cut and enjoy!
