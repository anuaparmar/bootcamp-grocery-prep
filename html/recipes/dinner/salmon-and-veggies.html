Salmon and Veggies

A simple and filling meal.

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 cup sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Add the veggies in a large sheet pan and season with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  3. Add the salmon fillets to the sheet pan.
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until the salmon is cooked at the veggies are tendered. Enjoy!
