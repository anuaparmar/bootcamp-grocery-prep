Veggie Wrap

A colorful and filling wrap.

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla (whole wheat, flour)
  • 1/2 sliced red onion
  • 1 sliced red pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 3 cups leafy greens (i.e. Romaine lettuce, Iceberg lettuce)
  • 1 tbsp hummus
  • 1/2 sliced avocado
  • 1 handful crumbled feta cheese
  • Honey balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Spread hummus on one side of the tortilla.
  2. Add the red onion, pepper, carrots, leafy greens, avocado, and feta cheese.
  3. Drizzle the honey balsamic vinegar, if desired. Fold the ends and roll. Enjoy!
