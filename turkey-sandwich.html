Turkey Sandwich

A classic and tasty sandwich.

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (i.e. multi-grain, white)
  • 1 tsp mayo
  • 3 slices of turkey
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 1 slice of cheese (i.e. cheddar cheese, pepper jack)

Instructions

  1. Spread mayo on both slices of bread.
  2. Add the turkey, tomato, lettuce, and cheese. Enjoy!
